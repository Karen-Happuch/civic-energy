Renter journey · Step 4

Subsidies finder

Most renters leave thousands on the table. Enter your ZIP and income range — we find every program you qualify for.

Subsidy finder tool ZIP code + income input → matched programs with apply links

Available programs

Federal

Residential Clean Energy Credit (ITC)

30% federal tax credit on your panel cost. Applies to renters who own their system. File on Form 5695.

CA State

SGIP Battery Storage Rebate

Up to $200/kWh for battery storage paired with solar. Income-based tiers available.

PG&E / SFPUC

Net Energy Metering (NEM 3.0)

Export excess power to the grid, receive credits on your monthly bill. Export pricing varies by time of day.

CA State

DAC-SASH Program

Up to $3/watt for residents in disadvantaged communities. No ownership required. Check eligibility by ZIP.

SB 868

Plug-In Solar Renter Rights

Not a rebate — a legal right. Landlords cannot refuse or charge extra for a compliant plug-in solar installation. [Verify current SB 868 implementation details]
